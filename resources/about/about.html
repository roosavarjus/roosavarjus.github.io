Who am I?

I am Finnish, 26 years old woman residing in Copenhagen who recently found her passion in web development! Below I tell you how.

I am originally a biomedical scientist, but took a turn towards computer science when I did my MSc degree in bioinformatics. I was always interested in designing and creating visually beautiful web pages, and when I heard about Shiny framework in R, I was sold. I quickly realized that I need to learn front-end knowledge to properly customise my web apps, and that's when I did the Full Stack developer career path in Codecademy. Now I am just so excited to learn all about creating the coolest web apps and landing a job in the field.
